# SapientAI — energy demand
# Vanilla behavior relocated, with ONE change: the scaling rail. Vanilla capped
# energy ambition at 100 — but building upkeep alone is 2/5/8 energy per tier
# (@b1/2/3_upkeep), so a mature empire pays hundreds in maintenance before a
# single ship. Rail raised to @sapient_energy_ceiling; still deliberately low
# relative to alloys/research (energy is a utility, not a goal), and machine
# empires' real energy demand rides the research/unity chain couplings, which
# are uncapped by design. Nomad variants untouched.

}
